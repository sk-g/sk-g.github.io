About Me

I lead responsible AI research for Q-IDE (previously - Amazon CodeWhisperer). I also research long context training and inference methods for large language models. Previously, I have built neural and statistical methods for machine translation and cross-lingual information retrieval at Raytheon BBN Technologies. I have over 5 years of experience in building and deploying complex machine learning solutions ranging from algorithm research to end to end pipeline design.

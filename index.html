About Me

I lead responsible AI research for Amazon CodeWhisperer. I also research long context training and inference methods for large language models. Previously I have built statistical and neural methods for machine translation and cross lingual information retrieval at Raytheon BBN Technologies. I have 5 years experience in building and deploying complex machine learning solutions and pipelines ranging from algorithm research to engineering design.
